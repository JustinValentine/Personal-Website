About

Welcome to my website, firstly I would like to give a short introduction of myself followed by a brief description of the sorts of projects that will be featured here.

My name is Justin Valentine, and I am currently studying math and computer science at the University of Alberta. As an inventor at heart, I find the most joy in life when I can take that initial leap from zero to one. For as long as I can remember, I have been obsessed with learning new skills, which has exposed me to a vast number of disciplines and has given me an atypical combination of skills. My goal is to integrate my knowledge of maths, computer sciences, physics, and business into a unique skill set, becoming my own flavor of polymath. I hope to inspire and created useful solutions along the way.
